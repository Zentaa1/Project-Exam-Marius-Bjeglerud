About Us

We are a car club based around Innlandet And Oslo Norway. Street Dreams started in 2022 by some car enthusiasts that wanted a group and a community to hang out with.

Street Dreams

Street Dreams members all have one thing in common, all cars stands out from the common cars you see on the Road. If you are located around where we are based and have a car that stands out, dont hesitate send us an application HERE.
